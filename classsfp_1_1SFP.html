Detailed Description

Class used for interpreting EEPROM values of SFP+ modules.

Has two lists of integers that represent the memory map of SFP/SFP+ modules. These are referred to by the I2C address of the memory, 0xA0 and 0xA2. These are sometimes referred to as 0x50 and 0x51, respective. The change is due to the 7-bit addressing supported by SFP+ modules.
